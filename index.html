Using calc with mixed units of measurement.

It's important to know the elements true width in the DOM. Calc's most usful property is its ability to mix units of measurement, like percentages, ems and pixels.

With calc(), you can subtract the margin, padding, and border depending on wether you have border-box or content-box.

#box{ width:calc(33.33333% - .5em - 30px); margin:.25em; padding:10px; border:5px solid pink; }

Width = 33.33333% +(margin x2)+(padding x2)+(border x2)
